Metropolis

From the sky reaching buildings of Tokyo megalopolis to fishing villages in Kumano region. I explore the skyscrapers, side streets, shopping malls, arcades and abandoned buildings. What I like the most about shooting in Japan is the fact that knowing no Japanese at all I’m never exactly at what am I looking at. Is a shop? Restaurant? Brothel? Hobby store or Ernesto Che Guevara themed fuck hole? It’s often impossible to tell. The advantage of that is as a gaijin I can get away with most things: trespassing, invading staff rooms, visiting used underwear store – I’ve done it all and then some. Just don’t tell anyone.
